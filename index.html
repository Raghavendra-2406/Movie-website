Synopsis

A deeply intimate romantic drama that follows the tender yet turbulent emotional journey of a married couple, Anand and Priya. Once deeply in love, their relationship begins to slowly unravel not because of a lack of affection, but because of the quiet, creeping distance that grows between them over time.

Told through a delicate interplay of past and present, the story reveals how small misunderstandings, unspoken emotions, and unhealed wounds can silently chip away at a once strong bond. As ego and emotional neglect take root, what was once a nurturing partnership becomes strained, leaving both Anand and Priya silently yearning for the connection they’ve lost.

It is not just a love story it’s a meditation on how two people can drift apart even while living under the same roof. With poignancy and restraint, the filmexplores the fragility of love, the weight of things left unsaid, and the heartbreak of realizing what was truly important only when it’s almost too late. The narrative builds gently toward a powerful and heart-wrenching revelation—one that forces both characters to confront the truths they’ve long avoided, and reshapes everything they thought they knew about each other.
